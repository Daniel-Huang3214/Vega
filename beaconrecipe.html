Beacon

The Beacon is a block that has multiple purposes, the main ones being the buffs and effects it gives to a player. The level 1 buffs being speed and regeneration, The level 2 buffs being resistance and jump boost, and finally the level 3 buffs being strength. To build and use a beacon you must first obtain its crafting ingredients, which are 5 Glass blocks, 3 Obsidian blocks, and a Nether Star. The Nether star is a drop from the Wither Boss. To actually activate a beacon you need to make a pyramid for the beacon to sit ontop of. The blocks that you can use are Iron blocks, Gold blocks, Diamond Blocks, Emerald Blocks, and Netherite blocks This consist of 9 blocks below it with the beacon on the centre top piece. 9 blocks is the first level, to obtain level 2 and 3 you require more layers adding onto the blocks. In total you would go from 9, 34, 83, to 164 blocks in total for each level. Another purpose for the beacon is as the name implies, a beacon. The beacon shines a ray of light in a straight line above it , this can be used to navigate back to a landmark or point you placed the beacon at.
