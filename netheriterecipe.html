Netherite Ingot

I absolutely hate this item, not because it's stronger than diamond, but it's because it's annoying to make. The amount of steps and material you need is absurd. Netherite is created from Ancient debris which is found in the nether which also has a 0.004% chance of spawning, fun game isn"t it. Whats even more fun is that you require a blast furnace, not a normal furnace to cook this thing. After thats done you just got netherite scrap. Now that thats done, you need to repeat it four more times for one bar of netherite. The recipe is 4 netherite scrap and 4 gold bars. The end product is a Netherite Ingot which you can combine diamond gear with to make netherite gear. This is one of the most tedious task in the game and is probably for endgame players.
