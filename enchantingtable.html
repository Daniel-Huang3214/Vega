Enchantment Table

The Enchantment Table has got to be one of my favourite blocks due to how useful it is. If you've seen the memes you might know about the enchanting table language which noone can understand. To use an enchanting table you need lapis lazui and exp levels. These are gained from mining the Lapis Ore you find in caves and exp is gained from basically anything. With these you can enchant weapons to buff them and increase their overall power. To craft it, you require four obsidian, two diamonds, and one book. The enchantment table can also recieve higher levels if you place bookshelves around it, this for some reason makes it more powerful but idk it's a game.
