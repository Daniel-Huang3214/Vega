Cake

Cake also known as diabetes 2, is one of the unique blocks due to how it can be used and also how hard it is to craft. Cake can be placed down and if your hunger bar is low, right clicking will instantly take 1/7 chunk out of it. If you spam your right mouse button, you can end up eating it in mere seconds you fat pig. Cakes are good for eating while running from something as you can place it infront of you and devour it in seconds like a fat pig. Cake's recipe requires a lot of items, 3 buckets of milk for some reason, 2 pieces of sugar, 3 wheat, and one egg. This cake is probably going to taste bad.
