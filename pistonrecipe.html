Piston

The piston is an item which redstone gods use like a part of their body. This block can be used to push other blocks forward once. Its counterpart Sticky piston can push and pull the block back while a normal piston can only push. This item is also something I find very annoying to craft as I never use it and I never will. To craft it you require 3 wood planks, 4 cobblestone blocks, 1 iron ingot, and 1 redstone dust.
